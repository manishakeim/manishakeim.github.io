research

Emotion Detection in Tweet Replies

Master's Thesis, Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women

Advisors: Dr. Rishabh Kaushal, Dr. Niyati Baliyan

Decoding Twitter's political pulse: This study employed automatic sentiment analysis of tweet replies to gauge public sentiment toward government policies on Twitter.

Community Detection in Signed Social Network

Bachelor's Thesis, University of Delhi

Advisors: Dr. Vinti Agarwal

Delving into the complex world of signed social networks, my research explored how to identify communities where connections can be both positive and negative. Using the combined power of modularity and genetic algorithms, I aimed to uncover hidden structures and relationships within these intricate networks.
